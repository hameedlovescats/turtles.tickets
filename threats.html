Turtle Threats

:Turtles face various threats that endanger their survival:

Pollution: Marine pollution, including plastic debris and chemical contaminants, harms turtles when they mistakenly ingest or become entangled in it.

Habitat loss: Destruction of nesting beaches and coastal development deprive turtles of suitable nesting sites and feeding grounds.

Illegal trade: Turtles are poached for their meat, shells, eggs, and other body parts, driving many species to the brink of extinction.
